Feijoada

A classic of our cuisine, feijoada is a dish that has some variations, but all of them are very delicious! This feijoada recipe we bring you is very traditional, containing meats such as pig ears, tails and feet. Check out how to make it and enjoy your lunch!

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of black beans
  • 100 g of dried meat
  • 70 g of pig ear
  • 70 g of pork tail
  • 70 g of pig's feet
  • 100 g of pork ribs
  • 50 g pork loin
  • 100 g of paio
  • 150 g Portuguese sausage

Steps

  1. Soak the meat for 36 hours or more, change the water several times, if it's a hot environment or summer, put ice on top or in cold layers.
  2. Cook step by step: hard meat, then soft meat.
  3. When soft, add the beans and remove the meat.
  4. Finally season the beans.
