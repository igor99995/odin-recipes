Strogonoff

Chicken stroganoff is a dish loved by everyone and very easy to prepare! How about learning how to make this delicious treat in your home? This chicken stroganoff recipe is so simple it will surprise you!

Ingredients

  • 3 chicken breasts cut into cubes
  • 1 clove of minced garlic
  • salt to taste
  • black pepper to taste
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 spoon of butter
  • 1/2 cup of ketchup
  • 1/3 cup of mustard
  • 1 cup of mushrooms
  • 1 glass of milk cream
  • straw potatoes to taste

Steps

  1. In a pan, mix the chicken, garlic, mayonnaise, salt and pepper.
  2. In a large frying pan, melt the butter and brown the onion.
  3. Add the seasoned chicken until it is golden.
  4. Add the mushrooms, ketchup and mustard.
  5. Add the cream and remove from the heat before boiling.
  6. Serve with white rice and potatoes chips.
