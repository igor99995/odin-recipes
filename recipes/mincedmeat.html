Even in the simplest recipes, there are secrets that can make all the difference! To prepare this sautéed ground beef recipe, you will have a step-by-step guide that will take your lunch to perfection! You'll even see how to season meat in a very easy way! Check out how to make this delicious treat now!

Ingredients

  • 500 g of ground beef
  • 1/2 onion
  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 2 crushed garlic cloves
  • 1 level tablespoon of tomato paste
  • coriander to taste
  • 1 level tablespoon of those complete seasonings that come ready
  • 1/2 cup of water

Steps

  1. In a pan, place the meat with the oil, 1 tablespoon of seasoning and the garlic
  2. Mix well and let it rest for 5 minutes.
  3. Then blend the onion and coriander with 1/2 cup of water in a blender.
  4. Then pour everything into the meat pan along with the tomato paste.
  5. Let it cook until it tastes good and the meat is cooked.
